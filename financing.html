Frequently Asked Questions

Does applying for financing affect my credit score?
No. Our initial pre-approval process uses a "soft pull" on your credit profile, which has zero impact on your credit score. A hard inquiry is only required once you finalize a vehicle purchase and officially sign the loan documents.
Can I use my own bank or credit union?
Absolutely. While we work with over 40 premium lenders to find you the best rate, you are always welcome to bring your own financing. We will provide you with all necessary documentation to facilitate a seamless transaction with your financial institution.
Can I roll my trade-in equity into the new loan?
Yes. Any positive equity from your trade-in vehicle can be applied directly toward your down payment, which typically results in a lower monthly payment and can help secure a more favorable interest rate.
